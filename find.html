How To Find Us!

Approaching by car from the M5, leave the motorway at junction 25, head towards Taunton and take the A358 for Minehead. When you reach Williton , turn left and take the A39 towards Minehead.


After approximately 8 miles, when you reach Alcombe, turn left into Brook Street and follow this road for 160 yards. We are situated on the corner of Manor Road and Combeland Road. If you are using a "sat nav" for guidance, our postcode is TA24 6EH.


The Britannia Inn is one of the oldest free houses in the Minehead area serving real ales in a traditional environment.
